Who We Are


Who We Are

Take a quick tour about Verbum Networks!

Verbum Networks is an Information and Communications Technology Solutions Provider, owned and managed by the Dominican Fathers and Brothers of Nigeria and Ghana. The firm is licensed by the Nigerian Communication Commission to provide for the general public, Wireless Internet Services.

As a corporate organization, Verbum Networks believes in providing services to the public so as to empower people to be creative and innovative in the way and manner they carry out their daily work, so as to engender performance-oriented results that will ensure our nation’s steady march towards human and economic development.

  • Our capabilities include: development of matchless portals and website solutions to increase the efficiency and competitive advantage of companies by enabling web technologies for their businesses; development of software of various kinds depending on the request of the clients; provision of internet connectivity through VSAT and fibre optics; setting up of small and large scale wireless networks; etc.
  • With a broad-based record of clients which cuts across corporate business organizations, homes, government establishments, educational institutions, individual and other Internet service providers, Verbum has not only wielded to itself an exceptionally fast growing clientele, it is on its way to redefining information technology delivery and performance.
  • With its motto of creativity, innovation and performance, Verbum has set a unique business standards of efficiency of delivery, reliability, competitive pricing and transparency. The company is manned by an exceptionally talented team of seasoned professionals.
