Our services

Services We Provide

Web & Software Development

We develop matchless website solutions to increase the efficiency and competitive advantage of companies, institutions and organizations by enabling web technologies for their businesses. We not only build websites, but also register domain names, and host websites so as to give your website that presence it needs on the internet 24/7.

Satellite Internet Access

Our VSAT internet is an Internet connection via satellite, which means it can be used anywhere in Nigeria, in remote locations, farms, villages, free trade zones, industrial parks and anywhere your imagination can take you. It is also very often installed in Cities for Enterprises, SMEs, or Home Users that are not satisfied with the 3G, 4G or Fiber network.


Networking and Wireless Hotspots

We plan and deploy internet access and services to homes, offices, schools, and organizations. We achieve this by implementing wireless networks, RF Technologies, WLAN securities, site survey and WiFi Hotspots Technology.


ICT Education (CICOT)

Through its Technical Education Unit (CICOT), Verbum Networks offers various ICT Research and Training programs. We are committed to providing qualitative and affordable IT education for organizations, businesses and all classes of individuals.


CCTV Surveillance/Intercom

We offer one of the best CCTV Security solutions for your properties. We offer complete computer-based CCTV/DVR, IP and WiFi surveillance systems, with security cameras at affordable rates. We also deploy PABX telephony system for effective communication within organizations.
